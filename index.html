About Me

I specialize in developing AI solutions across diverse industries.

I completed my MSc in Artificial Intelligence at AIIM Lab, Queen's University, where I developed self-supervised learning algorithms for ECG data analysis, contributing to publications in IEEE journals. My work focused on enhancing cross-dataset generalization and optimizing data augmentations for contrastive learning. Prior to that, I earned my BEng in Electrical Engineering from the University of Tehran, where I gained valuable experience working in various labs and on diverse projects, including brain blood flow prediction using deep learning approaches, and took many foundational courses that marked the beginning of my AI journey. I have held research roles at institutions like the Vector Institute, where I joined as a Machine Learning Associate (MLA) and worked on forecasting models for seafood processors. My research has been featured in conferences like IJCNN and published in the IEEE Journal of Biomedical and Health Informatics (JBHI), and I continue to explore innovative AI applications across various fields.
